How a bond forms

Three steps, one strong seam

01 · Apply

Spread thin, spread even

A thin, uniform layer beats a thick, uneven one — that's true of every adhesive we make, from wood glue to fabric fix.

02 · Press

Close contact, no gaps

Clamp it, weigh it down, or hold it firm. The bond forms where the two surfaces actually touch.

03 · Cure

Give it the time it needs

Every product's pack tells you its set time and full-cure time. Rushing this step is the most common reason a joint fails.
