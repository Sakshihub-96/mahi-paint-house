Our story

Started in a workshop, not a lab

Sanjog began in 1974 when a small carpentry supplier in Kanpur got tired of adhesives that let go too soon. Fifty years later, we still test every formula the same way — on a real workbench, under real load.

1974

A carpenter's complaint

Our founder ran a timber and hardware shop, and heard the same complaint from every carpenter who walked in: the glue held for a week, then let go. He started mixing his own batches in the back room.

1989

Beyond wood

Tailors and hobbyists started asking for the same reliability for fabric and paper. We built dedicated formulas instead of stretching one glue to do every job.

2006

Moisture-resistant line

Outdoor furniture makers needed a bond that could survive monsoon humidity without weakening. MR Exterior Bond became our best-selling formula within two years.

Today

Twelve formulas, one standard

Every batch, whether it's headed for a school supply shelf or a furniture factory, is tested on the material it's built for — not just in a beaker.

What we hold to

Three things that don't change between batches

Tested on the bench, not just the beaker

Lab results tell you half the story. We check every formula against the actual material — timber, cotton, card — before it ships.

One formula, one job

We'd rather make twelve honest specialists than one glue that claims to do everything and does nothing especially well.

The pack tells the truth

Set time, cure time, and coverage on the label match what happens on your workbench — no rounding up.
